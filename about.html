About us

Founded by visionary aviation enthusiast Hans Andersson, GP Aviation has quickly risen to prominence in the helicopter industry. Hans, driven by his lifelong passion for aviation, established GP Aviation with a singular mission: to provide unparalleled helicopter experiences to enthusiasts and travelers alike. Hans's journey began as a helicopter pilot himself, and his dedication to safety, customer satisfaction, and innovation has been the cornerstone of GP Aviation's success. The company has built a reputation for its exceptional service, top-notch pilot team, and a commitment to making the thrill of helicopter flight accessible to everyone. From scenic tours to luxury transfers, GP Aviation has redefined the way people experience the world from above. With a fleet of meticulously maintained helicopters and a team of skilled professionals, Hans's vision of sharing the magic of flight has become a reality. At GP Aviation, we believe that the sky is not the limit—it's just the beginning of your adventure. Join us and let your dreams take flight!
