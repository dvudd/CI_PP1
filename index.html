Important information

Safety is our top prority, We understand that your enjoyment and peace of mind go hand in hand. That's why we go above and beyond to ensure your safety throughout every moment of your helicopter adventure. Our fleet of helicopters is meticulously maintained and inspected by certified aviation professionals, adhering to the strictest industry standards. Our experienced and highly-trained pilots have an impeccable safety record, with years of expertise in navigating the skies. Before each flight, passengers receive a comprehensive safety briefing, where we explain the emergency procedures and provide all necessary safety equipment. Rest assured, your seat is equipped with state-of-the-art safety harnesses, and our helicopters are equipped with the latest in aviation safety technology. We continuously monitor weather conditions to guarantee safe flying conditions, and if conditions are unfavorable, we won't hesitate to reschedule your tour to a safer time. Your safety is our commitment, allowing you to relax and fully savor the awe-inspiring beauty of Stockholm and its archipelago from the comfort of our secure and well-maintained helicopters.
