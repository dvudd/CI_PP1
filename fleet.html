Our Fleet

Sikorsky S-76D

Stockholm, Gothenburg, Gävle

The Sikorsky S-76D is a versatile and advanced medium-sized twin-engine helicopter designed for various missions, including corporate and executive transport, offshore oil and gas operations, and emergency medical services. It is known for its exceptional performance, featuring powerful Pratt & Whitney Canada PW210S engines, advanced avionics, and a spacious and comfortable cabin. The S-76D offers impressive speed, range, and reliability, making it a popular choice for those seeking a high-quality and dependable helicopter for a range of applications.

Robinson R44

Gävle

The Robinson R44 is a popular and widely-used light helicopter renowned for its excellent suitability as a learner helicopter. With its simple and responsive controls, it provides an ideal platform for aspiring pilots to develop their flying skills. The R44 is prized for its affordability, ease of maintenance, and versatility, making it a preferred choice for flight training, aerial photography, and light utility missions. Its robust and dependable design, coupled with a comfortable cabin for up to four occupants, has made it a standout option for those starting their helicopter pilot journey.
